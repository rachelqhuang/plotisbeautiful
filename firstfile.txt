#qplot:

This function allows you to uncover some basic plotting power of ggplot2.
Here is the function: 
	qplot(x, y, data=, color=, shape=, size=, alpha=, geom=, method=, formula=, facets=, xlim=, ylim= xlab=, ylab=, main=, sub=)

	x and y represent the x and y axis. For univariate data, like histogram, we dont need y because y will be count of the rows.
